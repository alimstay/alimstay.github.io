Alim STAY Official FAQ

We answer frequently asked questions about staying at our unmanned hotel. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us via our official LINE account.

1. About Alim STAY

Alim STAY is a new style of unmanned hotel with no permanent front desk staff. You can smoothly complete self-check-in and check-out using your own smartphone or tablet. This operating style allows us to offer you a more reasonable and flexible stay.

Please don't worry. For any questions or concerns during your stay, we offer chat support via our official LINE account. Our dedicated support team will assist your stay remotely.

2. Check-in / Check-out

Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is until 11:00 AM.

Please complete the check-in procedure using the "Check-in Guide" message in the message box of each OTA site sent to you after booking is confirmed. No procedure is required at a front desk.

If you arrive before check-in time, you will not be able to enter the guest rooms or lounge. You will be able to use both the lounge and guest room once you receive the unmanned check-in guide.

We apologize, but as an unmanned hotel, we cannot store luggage before check-in or after check-out.

Simply leave your room by the check-out time (11:00 AM). No special procedures are required.

3. Contact & Support

Please contact us via chat through our official LINE account. We prioritize chat support to accurately understand your situation and respond promptly.

We apologize, but we generally do not provide phone support. We have consolidated our support system to chat to pass on the cost savings from unmanned operations to our guests' accommodation rates. However, we do have a phone support channel available, which is a supplementary service limited to emergency situations. Therefore, even during business hours, staff may not be able to answer the phone immediately. We appreciate your cooperation in using chat for inquiries as much as possible.

Chat support is available from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. For emergencies outside these hours, please refer to Section 7 "Trouble / Emergency."

4. Wi-Fi & Facilities

Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. Please check the check-in guide for the SSID and password.

We apologize, but there are no shared laundry facilities in the building. Please use a nearby coin laundry.

The hotel is entirely non-smoking. Smoking, including e-cigarettes and heated tobacco products, is strictly prohibited in guest rooms and common areas.

5. Amenities & Cleaning

We provide toothbrush, toothpaste, razor, cotton, cotton swabs, disposable slippers, eco-friendly reusable sandals, shampoo, conditioner, body soap, bath towel, and face towel.

â€» Towels are provided one face towel and one bath towel per person per night.

For multi-night stays, cleaning is provided as eco-cleaning service. Fresh sheets are provided from the 4th night onwards.

â€» Staff will not enter the room until the 3rd night.

Please use the trash bins in your room or the lounge. We appreciate your cooperation in sorting your trash.

6. House Rules

Non-guests are strictly prohibited from entering. If entry by more than the number of people booked is confirmed, additional charges may be applied.

We prioritize comfortable stays. After 9:00 PM, please be especially considerate of loud conversations and the volume of televisions and music. Please refrain from any behavior that may disturb neighboring residents or other guests.

We apologize, but pets are not allowed.
